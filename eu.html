About Edified Ummah

Edified Ummah was established in 2020 as an initiative to provide authentic Islamic knowledge to Muslims worldwide. In an age of misinformation and extremist ideologies, we aim to be a beacon of traditional Islamic understanding based on sound scholarship and authentic sources.

Our name "Edified Ummah" reflects our mission: to build up (edify) the Muslim community (Ummah) through knowledge, wisdom, and spiritual growth. We believe that authentic knowledge is the foundation for a balanced approach to Islam that emphasizes both the letter and spirit of the religion.

Through our podcast, study circles, online courses, and community activities, we strive to make traditional Islamic knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or previous exposure to Islamic studies.

Our Mission & Vision

Authentic Knowledge

To revive and disseminate authentic Islamic knowledge through reliable sources and qualified scholars with proper chains of transmission (isnad), countering misinformation and baseless innovations.

Balanced Approach

To promote a balanced understanding of Islam that integrates both the outward practices and inner dimensions of faith, avoiding extremes of rigid literalism and baseless innovation.

Community Building

To foster a supportive community of knowledge seekers who encourage each other in learning, practice, and spiritual growth, creating spaces for meaningful connections and collaboration.
